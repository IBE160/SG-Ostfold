ibe160 - Design Direction Showcase

Here are different design directions for the key screens of the ibe160 application. Each direction explores variations in layout, information hierarchy, and interaction patterns, all built upon our chosen "Modern & Tech-Forward (Dark)" theme. Review these mockups to see how different approaches feel and which best aligns with our core principles of **Speed, Clarity, Frictionless Data Entry, and Insights, Not Just Data**.

1. Dashboard: Sidebar Nav, Data-Dense

Philosophy: Professional & Efficient. Designed for power users who need quick access to many metrics. Uses a fixed sidebar for navigation and a dense card-based layout for KPIs.

2. Shift Report: Single Column, Guided

Philosophy: Simple & Focused. Guides the Shift Leader through the report step-by-step. Ideal for ensuring all required data is captured without overwhelming the user.

3. Dashboard: Top Nav, Spacious, Visual

Philosophy: Modern & Approachable. Uses a top navigation bar for a cleaner, more spacious feel. Emphasizes visual clarity with larger KPI cards and prominent charts.

4. Shift Report: Two Column, Quick Entry

Philosophy: Fast & Efficient. Designed for experienced Shift Leaders to quickly fill out reports. Uses a two-column layout to minimize scrolling and keep related fields visible.

New Shift Report

5. Historical Reports: Filter-First, Table

Philosophy: Data-Driven & Analytical. Prioritizes filtering and a clear, sortable table view for managers to quickly find specific reports and export data.
